Restaurant Description.
Conformity is the greatest enemy toward garment cuisine in the hotdog industry. Here at Sizzling Hotdogs we combat this enemy by having our customers suggest changes to our menu. Each menu item rotates every six months in order to ensure fresh and new creations can be explored. But, don't worry if you have a favorite that is no longer on the menu, you can special request this item for a small fee. Each ingredient used is decided by quality and not by price. Since Sizzling Hotdogs is committed to non- conformity our restaurant is situated in the middle of a residential area. This home/business helps customers feel more relaxed in a traditional setting, like eating a meal at home. So, come down to Sizzling Hotdog for your dog today. Remember that parking is extremely limited in residential areas.
